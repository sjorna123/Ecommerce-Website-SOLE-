About Us

Our story, our passion for soles

Welcome to SOLE

SOLE is more than just a shoe store; it's a lifestyle. Founded in 2020 in Baneshwor, Nepal, we started with a simple passion for footwear that combines style, comfort, and durability. Today, we are proud to serve customers across Nepal with premium shoes for every occasion — from daily wear to sports and fashion.

Our team carefully selects materials and partners with trusted manufacturers to ensure every pair meets the highest standards of quality and comfort. We believe the right shoes can change the way you walk through life.

Why Choose SOLE?

• Premium quality materials sourced responsibly

• Trendy and timeless designs

• Affordable prices with up to 50% off on special collections

• Fast delivery across Nepal

• Dedicated customer support 24/7
